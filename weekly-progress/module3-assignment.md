## Part A: Deep Dive Resource Analysis

### Resource 1: Academic Research - Optimal Feature Vectorization

**Citation:** Tamal, M. A., Islam, M. K., Bhuiyan, T., Sattar, A., & Prince, N. U. (2024). Unveiling suspicious phishing attacks: Enhancing detection with an optimal feature vectorization algorithm and supervised machine learning. *Frontiers in Computer Science, 6*, 1428013.

**Key Findings:**
- Tested on 274,446 URLs (134,500 malicious, 139,946 legitimate)
- Random Forest achieved **97.52% accuracy** (highest of all algorithms tested)
- Identified 10 novel URL characteristics for detection
- Emphasized importance of feature engineering

**Relevance:** 5/5 - Provides empirical benchmarks and validates algorithmic approach

---

### Resource 2: Industry Implementation - Cloudflare

**Citation:** Cloudflare. (2024). How Cloudflare is using automation to tackle phishing head on. *Cloudflare Blog*.

**Key Findings:**
- Improved automation from 37% to 78%
- Reduced response time from 3.4 days to under 1 hour
- Combined ML classifiers with threat intelligence and rules
- Emphasized "human-in-the-loop" approach

**Relevance:** 4/5 - Shows real-world scalability and deployment challenges
